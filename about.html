Clara de la Lama Laviada

Bio

Clara de la Lama Laviada is a multi-faceted creative force hailing from the vibrant cultural landscape of Mexico. Her journey in education and the arts has been an exploration of the human experience through the lenses of literature, theater, film, and language. With a profound curiosity for the world around her, Clara's diverse background has shaped her into a dedicated artist, dedicated to bridging cultural gaps and expressing the nuances of life through various mediums.

Clara embarked on her educational journey at the Universidad Iberoamericana in Mexico City, where she immersed herself in the rich tapestry of Latin American Literature. This experience ignited her passion for storytelling and set the stage for her future endeavors. Alongside her literary pursuits, Clara furthered her knowledge through specialized courses, including film history and essay writing, at the renowned Casa del Lago UNAM.

Clara's academic pursuits also led her to the UK, where she pursued a Sociology BSc (Hons) at the University of Bath. Here, she expanded her horizons and delved into the complexities of human interaction and societal dynamics. Moreover, her time at the Central School of Speech and Drama in London, where she completed an Introduction to Directing short course, marked her commitment to channeling her creative energy into the realm of theater.
